If you are a Classics student in Massachusetts, you can use our map below to find a local chapter near you! If your town has a fully opaque JCL icon, you can click on it to be taken to its website! If the icon is semi-transparent, your JCL may not have a website yet, and clicking will allow you to contact the chapter chair for more information. Websites and chapter chair contacts not listed here may be found in the MassJCL Local Chapter Directory (https://www.massjcl.org/local-chapters/).
